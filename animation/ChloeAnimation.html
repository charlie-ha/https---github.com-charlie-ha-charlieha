Chloe Saves The Day Animation

Project type:

Animation project in Maya.

Project description:

Animate a scene in which multiple characters interact.

Tell a story. Aim for around 40 - 60 seconds of action, with a heavy focus on refining the animation to make it smooth and believable.

-----------

Chloe is taking a rest in her apartment when an explosion happened, she quickly puts on her cloak of levitation, creates a portal, and teleports to the scene to stop the fire. Then she flies back and a loop begins.

Date:

Nov 10 - Dec 23 2024

Role:

3D Animator, Storyboard.

Project highlight:

Reflection:

The later part of the animation, where Chloe flies out to extinguish the fire, is faster than I anticipated. This makes the animation happen too fast and unimpressive. I wanted to create a heroic aura when Chloe arrives at the scene to stop the fire. I could have spaced the keyframes more to make the animation more realistic.
