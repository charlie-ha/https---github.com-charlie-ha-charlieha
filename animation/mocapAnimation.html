Motion Capture Animation

Project type:

Motion Capture Animation project in Maya.

Project description:

Imported and refined motion-captured animation data in Autodesk Maya to create a seamless character performance inspired by Doctor Strange’s portal-opening scene.

Retargeted MoCap data to a custom character rig, ensuring accurate skeletal mapping and proportions.

Integrated subtle secondary animations such as fingers and hand movements for realism and personality.

Date:

October 27 - November 10 2024

Role:

Motion Capture Animator.

Project highlight:

Reference:

Doctor Strange opens portal hand gestures.
