CHARLIE HA

What People Think Chess Is About Animation

Project type:

Facial Animation project in Maya.

Project description:

Animate a few seconds of speech and facial expression.

Date:

Oct 16 - Oct 27 2024

Role:

3D Animator.

Project highlight:

Reference:

A chess meme "What people think Chess is about"

Video used for facial expression.

Storyboard:

Identify and outline important narrative scenes and mark with time stamps.

Reflection:

Regarding the animation, I think the second part could have been smoother by making the character lean back more(like the reference video). I received a feedback from my professor that the hand motion of the first part is a bit distracting. I also think the hand motion need to be less to give more focus on the mouth movements.

I wanted to create a short animation to show everyone that chess is fun and interesting. Therefore, I picked a chess meme that I think many people who are unfamiliar with chess would understand and find this funny. However, when I show the animation to my friends, the animation appeals to chess players more than people outside of chess.
