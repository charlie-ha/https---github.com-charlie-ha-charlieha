Food Photography

I have a passion for food photography, particularly capturing the artistry of cakes. Through my lens, I've honed my skills in visual design, composition. The meticulous attention to detail required in highlighting the intricate layers and textures of each cake has equipped me with a keen eye—an essential asset in game design. In both food photography and game design, the creativity and innovation that define my approach to food photography are qualities I bring into my games major. This is the place where I strive to craft unique and compelling digital experiences.

Exploring the intersection of these two worlds has not only enriched my visual storytelling capabilities but also heightened my appreciation for the seamless blend of art and technology in creating captivating narratives.
