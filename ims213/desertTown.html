CHARLIE HA

Desert Town

Project type:

Third Person Character Murder Mystery Game in Unreal Engine.

Game description:

Step into the boots of a lone sheriff in a sun-scorched western town...

A brutal murder has rocked the heart of a once-quiet desert outpost. With only 10 minutes before the killer escapes, you must search the dusty streets, follow the trail of clues, and avoid deadly traps set to keep you off the case. TNT, accomplices, and ticking time bombs stand in your way — but justice waits for no one.

💰 Collect coins to bribe for secrets.

🔍 Gather evidence to unlock the truth.

🧨 Avoid traps — or die trying.

🚂 Use minecarts to reach hidden areas.
