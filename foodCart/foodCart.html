CHARLIE HA

3D Model: Food Cart

The assignment was to model a Food Cart. I modeled it to have a storage (by using extrude and pushed it in), drawers, and sauce bottles. For the mixing bowl, tissue plates and hand tissue box, I used the curve tool to draw out the shape of the bowl and revolved it. In the background, there is a table area with umbrellas and trash cans. I modeled the umbrella by modelling a sphere and delete the vertices to create the ridges, I went into edge mode to move out the edges of the umbrella to imitate the ribs of the umbrella.
