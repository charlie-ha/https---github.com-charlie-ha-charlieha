Pulley Tower

Project type:

Modeling and Texturing Project in Maya.

Project description:

In Spring 2023, I took an introductory class to Maya. The midterm project was to model a building in Miami University so I chose Pulley Tower.

I believe that Pulley Tower give a beautiful glow at night so I wanted to recreate that effect. However, within the limited time, I managed the model and texture.
