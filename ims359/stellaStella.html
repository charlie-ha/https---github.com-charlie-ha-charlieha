Project description:

This project, I crafted a captivating music video utilizing my skills in creative editing, and sound mixing. Understand that music videos often blend narrative storytelling with montage techniques.

I chose the song Stellar Stella because it has storytelling elements that I can present visually and the official music video has a lot of potential to push the song further.

Reflection:

This project helps me practice visualizing lyrics and and deepen my understanding of how music and imagery can work together to convey meaning. I reinterpreted the original English translation of the lyrics to better reflect the song’s emotional depth, enhancing the storytelling aspect through my visuals.

One of the key challenges I encountered was managing the project’s scope within a limited timeframe. I had to find a balance between the level of detail in each individual visual and the total number of visuals I could produce. This experience helped me refine my time management skills and taught me how to prioritize creative decisions for maximum impact.
